Welcome to Shomrei Hachomos Orthodox Chapel, Inc.

We understand the profound loss you are experiencing and are here to support you with compassion and care. We appreciate you entrusting us with the honor of serving your family during this difficult time. Our dedicated team is committed to providing a comforting environment and personalized services to honor your loved one’s memory while adhering to Orthodox Jewish traditions and rituals in our modern, clean facility.

To help us understand your needs and provide the best possible service, please take a moment to fill out our intake form. This will allow us to assist you more effectively and ensure that every detail is handled with the utmost care and respect.
